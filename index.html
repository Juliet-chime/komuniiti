What is KomunitiiSquare?

Central to the health of any democracy is citizens talking with one another and sharing their thoughts about politics and public affairs.

KomunitiiSquare is both a physical and virtual space where young Nigerians can engage in civic discourse around social, political, cultural or economic issues that affect them with the aim of participating in finding solutions.

Priming for the Future

The desired future is built on a system that works, as such our focus is on promoting new thinking and new alternatives to existing challenges.
