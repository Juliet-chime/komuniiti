Bringing Citizens Together

The Square gives a platform for civic discourse on key social, economic and cultural issues, allowing a more educated and engaged citizenry.

About Komunitii Square

Komunitii Square is a social action community - hub dedicated to organizing virtual and offline- communities to work together and explore possibilities for a positive change in the socio-economic fortunes of ordinary Nigerians and Nigeria itself - its whole and its parts.

Given the need for connecting people, Komunitii square(is):

Mindful

that by bringing people together Komunitii Square will ensure that the whole community can listen, connect, talk, re-think, and be motivated and to take action on its common concerns, overcome problems, and with a collective spirit work for a better Nigeria;

Believing & Focus

on the highest stakeholders in the nations future, the youth of Nigeria - over 70 percent of its population;

Convinced

that with awareness leading to realization, readiness, and responsiveness, communities can achieve renewal;

Assured that

Everyone has the ability to act!
Lets Think

Giving Voice, Building Citizen Change makers

When individuals unite and have the opportunity for civil discourse they can better raise awareness of community problems and be advocates for change.

Komunitii TownHalls:

By leveraging digital and social channels we are bringing together key players within the community to discuss issues and collective threats that they may face as well as proffering solutions to existing issues and challenges.

Digital Engagement Sessions

Our digital engagement sessions happen through IG live sessions that are hosted periodically as well as through Twitter Q n A sessions where subject matter experts are brought in to engage the Komunitii on trending topics and on broad issues affecting our collective future.
Let's Chat

Amplifying Marginalized Voices

People who subsist on the outer edges of a community have little or no chance of being seen or heard, without democracy, women, young people, ethnic minorities may be marginalized. Our platform empowers people to raise their voices and let their opinions be known.

Komunitii Podcast:

The komunitii podcast presents a way to use conversations to raise awareness on key issues as well as drive participation with an engaged citizenry, it offers up the opportunity to also communicate alternative approaches to existing issues and challenges existing norms and practices.

Social Conversations:

Our social conversations happen on a daily basis across the Komunitii social media platforms and it helps break down complex social, economic, political and cultural issues into easily digestible byte size formats to enlighten and educate the citizenry

Advancing the Role of Youth!

An engaged youth population drives economic growth, prosperity and democracy. By helping to identify barriers and opportunities to promote active participation, we help enlighten and educate young people around the real and present danger to their future as well as their roles in providing solutions.

Poverty elimination
Empowerment: Making Voters choice count
Quality public health
Localism as a core local development paradigm
Soft power as driver of productivity
Security of food systems and building climate resiliency
